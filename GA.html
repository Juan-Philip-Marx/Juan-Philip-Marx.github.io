What are Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWTs)

There are several different types of Vertical Axis Wind Turbines, But they are typically split into the aerodynamic force that drives them i.e. Lift based or Drag based. The Savonius turbine shown is drag based (the drag on the blade generates the tangential force to create movement), whereas the other 3 are lift based turbines. Lift based VAWTs have an array of issues that plague them including poor power generation in comparison to HAWTs, deep stall, dynamic stall and blade fatigue from inconsistent blade loading.
